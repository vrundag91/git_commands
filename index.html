What is E-mail Course

A customized training conducted via E-mail, which covers all the aspects of the subject according to your understanding and working style. Total course content is divided into series of E-mails. Each and every session will be followed by relative exercises.

Apart from these, each and every e-mail tutorial will have a video task, where students can learn different effects and tools of respective software.
